Description

My favorite hobby is dance. I like many different styles of dance, I like waacking, hiphop, urban, and jazz. Dancing is my favorite hobby and favorite interest. Dancing can makes me feel happy and relax. I love to listen to music and dance within the music. I've learned dancing for almost 4 years. I'm better at waacking now, because I go to a teacher's class every two weeks to practice my skills. Dancing is really fun but you need to work hard in order to be better and improve. Dance is not easy, it needs many skills and body foundation to perform a good and beautiful movement. i went to many competitions before, including Hero For Who, HHI, and Hiphop Kids International. I like to perform in front of others and do my best in front of the audiences. Even though we need a lot of practice for the competitions, I think I've learned a lot from all these experiences. I hope I can dance forever. Nothing can replace it!!
